About Ladakh And Names Of All Religious And Non Religious Popular places of Ladakh

Ladakh, India's high-altitude "Land of Passes," is famous for stark beauty, Buddhist monasteries, and Tibetan culture, offering adventure like trekking and scenic drives to places like Pangong Lake, Nubra Valley, and high passes showcasing its unique landscape and rich Buddhist heritage.
