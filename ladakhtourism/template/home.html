Travel Tips

  1. Acclimatize to the high altitude gradually.
  2. Carry warm clothing as temperatures can drop significantly.
  3. Respect local customs and traditions.
  4. Stay hydrated and avoid alcohol.
  5. Plan your travel during the best season (May to September).
